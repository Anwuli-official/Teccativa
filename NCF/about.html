About Us



The Nile Christian fellowship is the christian fellowship of Nile University of Nigeria. It was founded in the year 2017 by a group of christian students that observed the need for a christian fellowship in the university. The foundation has gradually grown over time and still in a process of constant growth strives to bring as many as possible to the Lord and convert as many lost souls as possible. learn more
